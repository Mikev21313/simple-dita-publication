Vehicle Specifications Guide

Standard specifications and measurements for automobile analysis.

Engine Specifications

Specification Unit Description
Displacement Liters/cc Total volume swept by all pistons
Horsepower HP/kW Maximum power output
Torque lb-ft/Nm Rotational force produced
Compression ratio Ratio Ratio of maximum to minimum cylinder volume
Bore x Stroke mm Cylinder diameter x piston travel distance
Redline RPM Maximum engine operating speed

Dimensions

Measurement Description
Wheelbase Distance between front and rear axle centers
Track width Distance between wheel centers on the same axle
Ground clearance Minimum distance between lowest point and ground
Overall length Total length from front to rear
Overall width Total width including mirrors
Overall height Total height from ground to highest point
Cargo volume Available storage space
Passenger volume Interior space for occupants

Performance Metrics

Metric Description
0-60 mph time Time to accelerate from standstill to 60 mph
Quarter-mile time Time to cover 1/4 mile from standstill
Top speed Maximum vehicle speed
Braking distance Distance to stop from specific speed
Lateral acceleration Maximum cornering capability (g-force)

Fuel Economy

Measurement Description
City MPG/L/100km Fuel economy in urban driving conditions
Highway MPG/L/100km Fuel economy in highway driving conditions
Combined MPG/L/100km Weighted average of city and highway figures
Fuel tank capacity Maximum fuel storage volume
Cruising range Maximum distance on a full tank
